Panniers and Montañas

Starting in Santander, heading South for warmer weather in Morocco, North to Iz’s brother’s wedding in Sweden, and finally (if we’re feeling sensible) back home to the UK.

We are Iz and Zoe. We are now full time cycle tourers. We are both vegan and concerned about our impact on the planet. Our journey is plant powered and flight free!

Many people have wanted to keep updated with our adventures and misadventures – we have created this blog for you! Please excuse the clunky design – we have never made a website before, and we are mainly doing this from our phones.

We’re aiming for one post every month over the year. We will be writing about the background and preparation for the trip, the highs and lows of cycle touring, and a sprinkle of bike nerdery for anyone interested!

We hope you enjoy reading. And please send us DMs! I would love to hear from you through WhatsApp / Signal / Telegram / even email.
